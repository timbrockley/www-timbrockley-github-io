Prices

Bespoke Packages

Bespoke packages, including discounted half day or full day rates, are available on request.

Prices are determined on an individual basis after discussion with the client.

Informal Shoots

Informal shoots from 1-2 hours are charged at £50 per hour + Travel Expenses.

This includes individual digital images, with basic editing, selected by the photographer.

Extras

£50 per hour at the photo shoot.

£10 per image for enhanced editing or retouching.

Studio costs are determined on an individual basis after discussion with the client.

Special Rates

If adding work to my own portfolio or working with models who have not yet created a portfolio, I am often happy to work on a TF basis.

For aspiring models adding to their portfolio, reduced rates vary from £20 per hour for simple assignments up to £40 for more complicated assignments involving more equipment (lighting etc).

In some cases I may still ask for travel expenses.
